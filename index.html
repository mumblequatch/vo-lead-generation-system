Target Niches (Priority Order)

  1. Explainer videos — 60-90 seconds, $250-400/video. SaaS companies churn through them constantly. Fastest path to money.
  2. E-learning — $0.15-0.25/word, projects run 5,000-50,000+ words. Longer game but recurring revenue once landed.
  3. Regional commercial — 15-60 seconds, $500-2,000+ per spot. Highest ceiling but more competitive.

PART 1: INITIAL SETUP (One-time, ~2-3 hours)

A. Apollo.io Setup

1. Create Account - Sign up at apollo.io - Connect your Gmail (for sending sequences) - Warm up your email for 1-2 weeks before heavy outreach (Apollo has built-in warmup)

2. Build Saved Searches

SEARCH 1: Explainer Video Companies (US) - Job Titles: Video Producer, Creative Director, Content Producer, Head of Video, Video Production Manager - Industry: Marketing & Advertising, Media Production, Internet - Keywords (company): “explainer video” OR “animated video” OR “product video” - Employee Count: 5-200 - Location: United States

SEARCH 2: Explainer Video Companies (International English) - Same filters as above - Location: United Kingdom, Canada, Australia

SEARCH 3: E-Learning / L&D Companies (US) - Job Titles: Instructional Designer, Learning & Development Manager, Training Manager, Director of Learning, Course Developer, L&D Director - Industry: E-Learning, Professional Training & Coaching, Human Resources - Keywords (company): “corporate training” OR “e-learning” OR “instructional design” - Employee Count: 10-500 - Location: United States

SEARCH 4: E-Learning / L&D Companies (International English) - Same filters as above - Location: United Kingdom, Canada, Australia

4. Save Sequences as Templates - Apollo lets you save these so you can enroll new leads with one click

B. Bodalgo Setup

1. Create Profile - Sign up at bodalgo.com - Upload demo reel clips (segment by type: commercial, narration, e-learning if you have separate cuts) - Fill profile completely—Bodalgo is curated, so quality matters

2. Set Notification Preferences - Get email alerts for new jobs matching your voice type - Check job board 3x/week minimum

3. Subscription - Start with Pro subscription (~$299/year) - ROI typically shows within 2-3 months if you audition consistently

C. Streak Setup (Gmail)

1. Install Chrome Extension - Add Streak from Chrome Web Store - Connect to your Gmail

2. Create Pipeline: “VO Submissions”

Researched → Contacted → Responded → Quoted → Booked → Delivered → Paid

Setup Period (Weeks 1-2)

PART 2: WEEKLY WORKFLOW

Total time: ~90 minutes/week once running

MONDAY: HARVEST (30 min)

The “get new leads” session

  1. Open Apollo
  2. Run your saved searches (they update automatically, but review fresh results)
  3. Review top 10-15 new leads per search
  4. Qualify each lead (60-second check):
    • Does their website show professional video/training work?
    • Do they list clients or portfolio?
    • Any sign they use external voice talent?
    • If yes → Add to sequence
    • If unclear → Skip (don’t waste outreach on maybes)
  5. Enroll qualified leads in appropriate sequence (Explainer or E-Learning)
  6. Apollo sends Email 1 automatically

That’s it. Apollo handles follow-ups on Day 7 and Day 21.

WEDNESDAY: AUDITION (30 min)

The “respond to inbound” session

  1. Check Bodalgo for new jobs matching your profile
  2. Pick 2-3 quality auditions (don’t spray and pray—selective)
  3. Before recording, verify:
    • Is the rate professional? (Check GVAA rate guide if unsure)
    • Is the client legit? (Company website, real contact info)
    • Does the read match your voice?
  4. Record and submit auditions
  5. Log each submission in Streak (Bodalgo label, “Contacted” stage)

FRIDAY: MAINTAIN (30 min)

The “keep the pipeline clean” session

  1. Open Streak
  2. Review cards in each column:
    • Responded: Anyone need a follow-up? Quote? Custom sample?
    • Quoted: Any quotes over 2 weeks old? Send gentle check-in
    • Booked: Any deliverables due?
    • Paid: Move to done, note for future reference
  3. Archive dead leads (no response after full Apollo sequence + 60 days)
  4. Personal follow-ups: For warm leads who responded but went quiet, send a personalized note (not automated)

PART 3: QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST

Before enrolling any lead, verify:

GREEN LIGHTS (proceed): - [ ] Website shows professional video/training production - [ ] Portfolio includes work with voiceover - [ ] Company size 5-200 employees (big enough to have budget, small enough to outsource) - [ ] Decision-maker title (Producer, Creative Director, L&D Manager—not intern) - [ ] Real company email domain (not Gmail)

RED FLAGS (skip): - [ ] No website or broken website - [ ] Only stock video / template work - [ ] Fiverr/Upwork shop - [ ] Pricing language suggests lowball (“budget-friendly,” “affordable”) - [ ] India/Philippines-based production (usually rate arbitrage)

PART 4: SCAM DETECTION

Immediate disqualifiers: - Overpayment + wire refund request (most common scam) - Gmail/Yahoo email claiming to represent established company - Interview request via Signal or Telegram - Above-market rates with “urgent” timeline - “Read without interpretation or emphasis” (AI training data harvesting) - Request for money from you for any reason

Golden rule: Never send money to or on behalf of a client. Ever.

PART 5: FOLLOW-UP CADENCE

Apollo automated sequence handles: - Day 0: Initial outreach - Day 7: First follow-up - Day 21: Final follow-up

After sequence completes (no response): - Day 60: Optional personal check-in - Day 90+: Add to quarterly “staying in touch” list or archive

For warm leads who responded but went quiet: - 2 weeks after last contact: Gentle check-in - 4 weeks: Value-add touch (relevant project, insight, or just “thinking of you”) - After 3 touches with no response: Archive and move on

Remember: 80% of sales happen after the 5th contact. Persistence pays.

PART 6: METRICS TO TRACK

Weekly glance (don’t obsess, just notice trends):

  • Leads enrolled this week: _____
  • Emails sent (Apollo shows this): _____
  • Response rate: _____%
  • Bodalgo auditions submitted: _____
  • Quotes sent: _____
  • Jobs booked: _____

Healthy benchmarks (industry average): - Cold email response rate: 5-15% - Quote-to-book rate: 20-40% - Bodalgo audition-to-book: varies widely, but 1 in 10-20 is normal

PART 7: RATE REFERENCE

Explainer Videos (60-90 sec): - Web only: $250-400 - Broadcast/paid media: $400-800+

E-Learning: - Per word: $0.15-0.25 - Per finished hour: $250-400 - Project rate (5,000 words): $750-1,250

Corporate Narration: - Per minute: $50-100 - Per finished hour: $300-500

Always check GVAA Rate Guide for current standards: globalvoiceacademy.com/gvaa-rate-guide-2/
